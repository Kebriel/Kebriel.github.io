My Promise

As a developer and a professional, I make the following promises as to my commitment to and approach to a collaborative workspace.

1) I am always learning, and it is always my priority to learn. I never believe that there is not a better way that I could do something, it's just a matter of whether I've found it yet 2) I am very project-oriented and I will work hard to see things through, regardless of how daunting they may be, and I work well in a team to accomplish larger tasks 3) As a bisexual and gender-questioning person, a safe and inclusive workspace is of paramount importance to me. Mutual respect and kindness between team members, and the upholding of a collaborative no-one-left-behind approach are both essential to me
