My Promise

As a developer and a professional, I make the following promises as to my commitment to and approach to a collaborative workspace.

  1. I am always learning, and it is always my priority to learn
  2. I am very project-oriented and I will work hard to see things through, regardless of how daunting they may be, and I work well in a team to accomplish larger tasks
  3. While I work well alone and am very used to doing so, I actually am very drawn to a team-centric/collaborative environment. Working with a team on a larger more complicated project is a natural space for me, I've just unfortunately not gotten the most chances to do it.
  4. As a bisexual and gender-questioning person, a safe and inclusive space is of paramount importance to me. Not only do I recognize the importance of those things, but I will always strive towards ensuring a space is safe and open for everyone -- and I'm not afraid to introspect upon my own behavior in pursuit of that goal either.
